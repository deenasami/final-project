Glamour Magazine's got the secret to a proposal -- or just a good old roasted chicken --
in their "100 Recipes Every Woman Should Know" cookbook. The story goes something like
this: a fashion editor at Glamour magazine once gave her assistant this recipe on how to
roast a chicken. The assistant made it for her boyfriend, who later popped the question. A
few other women tried it and, behold, got the same results. Who knew it was that easy!?
So my faithful reader(s?), I feel indebted to share the same recipe with you. I can't
personally attest to the guaranteed-proposal-test -- but I did change the recipe up a bit,
